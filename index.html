about my business

PasToGo was made by a local lakelander who LOVES pasta, but doesn't always want to go out to restaurants to get it. She was always on the go and wanted a fast and easy way to get restaurant quality pasta without having to dine in a nice venue. After years of dealing with this delema... she decided it was time to open up her own shop.
